💡 How Taxi Seat Pricing Works:
  • Front Seat (F1) — sits beside the driver. Usually set higher as a premium position.
  • Rear Seats (R1–R3 Sedan / R1–R5 SUV) — standard shared passenger seats.
  • Section 2 per-seat overrides always win over Section 1 defaults.
  • Each route can have completely independent pricing.
